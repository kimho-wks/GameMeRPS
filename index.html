RPSGame

There are 3 options: Rock, Scissors, and Paper. If you choose Rock, you will beat Scissors but lose to Paper. If you choose Paper, you will beat Hammer and lose to Scissors. If you choose Scissors, you will beat Paper and lose to Hammer.
